About Us

Get to know us better Instruments was born in 2010 creating a different business within the market of musical instruments in Spain. Pioneering chain in franchise of musical instruments and accessories, Instruments starts selling its products through its (online) store Instruments.com, revolutionizing a market that until then had not been able to reconcile quality, price, service and profitability. His business has focused on offering a range of over 7 thousand references of the best imported and domestic brands, with special emphasis on their own brands, such as Memphis. The two main advantages that characterize Instruments are a wide stock of a great diversity of brands at very competitive prices and in a counseling service for all audiences (beginners, amateurs, professionals, teachers, schools, institutions, etc.), but with greater dedication in the world of children and youth. There is a wide range of services in their daily activity, since the specialized sale is joined by a Lutheria activity and general repair, a music school, specific events and courses. The circle closes with a network of stores and a logistics service that allows the customer to receive their order normally in about 24-48 hours after having made it. Physical Stores In 2011, the first Instruments physical store was created in San Sebastián de los Reyes, where the offices and the central store are currently located. Its location has allowed all orders to be managed effectively within its facilities. Musical Instrument Franchises Instruments begins its expansion as a franchise of musical instruments once it has experienced success in its own establishments, making available to its franchisees a business model of success and unique and differentiated management. Instruments currently has music stores distributed throughout Spain: Alcorcón, Alcalá de Henares, Madrid, Ponferrada, San Sebastián de los Reyes and Zaragoza. The key year of consolidation of the Instruments Expansion Plan in its broadest sense was in 2015 due to: the construction of the franchise channel at the national level, the sizing of compact and efficient central services, the design and implementation of new services and the constant improvement of our product catalog. Full Growth Nowadays Instruments continues in full growth thanks to its revolutionary model of Instruments franchises, which work in unison to perfect their business model with a single goal: the full satisfaction of the music lover.
